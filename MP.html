💡 How to generate rare names:
Rare names combine unexpected word roots (Latin, Greek, Swahili, Japanese) with short syllables (≤3), avoid common suffixes like -ify, -hub, -mart, and carry a double meaning. Coin new words by blending two concepts — e.g. "Velo" (speed) + "Ora" (time) = Velora. Aim for names that are easy to spell, say aloud, and Google.
